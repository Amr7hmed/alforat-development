About Us

Alforat has cemented its place as the leading residential and gated community developer Alexandria, Egypt. We take pride in our innovative, sustainable and socially responsible approach to the shaping of integrated communities .

With years of experience in the market, we are committed to an unwavering vision of perfection and attention to every little detail in everything we do creating higher standards in the real estate market in Alexandria.

Vision

We aspire to be at the forefront of Egypt’s real estate market through the continued sustainable growth of brand equity, market share and prominent equitable relationship with our community. We are keen to develop our construction projects on a large scale.

Mission

Our mission is to keep refining our developments to sustain incomparable customer satisfaction. We strive to have a positive and lasting impact on Alexandria’s environment through a socially responsible residential and commercial approach. We are committed to having our excellent standards implemented in the stages of planning, design, execution and delivery.
